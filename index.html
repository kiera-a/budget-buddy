Why Budget Bunny?

Small school food purchases can add up quickly. Budget Bunny helps students stay aware before their allowance runs out.

💸

Problem

Students often spend on meals, drinks, and snacks without realising how much they have used in a week.

💬

Easy Logging

Instead of opening a budgeting app, students can simply type their spending in Telegram like a normal chat.

🐰

Friendly Support

Budget Bunny gives simple reminders and replies in a friendly tone, making budgeting feel less stressful.

🍽️

Student-Focused

The chatbot is designed around school meal spending, so it feels more relevant to RP students.
